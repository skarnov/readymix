    READYMIX CONCRETE (B) SDN BHD

    PRODUCTS & SERVICES

    Readymix is the leading concrete manufacturer and raw materials supplier in Brunei Darussalam with the largest fleet of heavy vehicles and batching plants.

    We are capable of providing on-site batching plants for large-scale projects to meet our client's specific requirements and religious production.

    REQUEST FOR QUOTATION

    CONCRETE MIX DESIGN

    Readymix is proud to cater to our client's requirements and specification to provide customized concrete mix design. Our computerized batching system linked to the ERP software is fast and provides accurate quality control and traceability ensuring mix design specifics. Our fly ash SCM is highly recognized by the Green Building Council of Brunei Darussalam.

    SUPPLY OF RAW MATERIALS

    Readymix has full control of aggregates through independent importation to ensure quality and consistency. Cement is acquired daily and directly from Butra Heidelberg Cement to accommodate major projects. Coarse aggregates are imported from Malaysia whereas fly ash are imported from India. Imports are endorsed by Badan Kemajuan Industri Brunei (BINA).

    RENTAL OF HEAVY VEHICLES

    Readymix has the largest fleet of mixer trucks, tipper trailers, and cement takers in Brunei Darussalam with capability to deploy machineries on short notice. Our vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking system to serve customers' requirements more effectively. We are also able to provide STL compliance mixer trucks to accommodate strict BSP orders.

    ABOUT READYMIX

    History

    Readymix is a second-generation family business established in 1962 by Mr. Ian Cocks in Adelaide, Australia. Since then, the establishment has expanded its operations throughout South East Asia, namely Brunei Darussalam, Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, and Vietnam.

    Quality Assurance

    Readymix is dedicated towards customer satisfaction and prioritizes the quality of its production and service. To ensure quality, Readymix is equipped with testing laboratories accredited by CPRU-Ministry of Development since 1994 and also ABCi certified.

    Mission

    Readymix aims to be the leading supplier of concrete and concrete raw materials in our markets by delivering quality products at competitive prices with professional service.

    Values

    Quality

    We will maintain our customers’ trust by delivering the best quality products which meet their expectations and set us apart in the market.

    Customer Service

    We will deliver the best possible experience to our customers by exceeding their expectations and keeping our promises.

    Continuous Improvement

    We will never stop striving to make everything better than before in order to enhance stakeholder experience.

    Sales Focused

    To grow and prosper, sales must be at the heart of our business.

    Teamwork

    We value our people above anything else. We are committed to their development and to appreciating their contribution. By working together we can achieve anything.

    Self Discipline

    Everyone has the right and responsibility to do what is right even when the rule is not there.

    MR. HAMIDDON HAJI MOHAMMAD SAID

    Mr. Hamiddon Said joined the Readymix team in 2019 as its Director after retiring from the Public Works Department (JKR) where he worked for over 27 years. Mr. Hamiddon graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Building Engineering under the Brunei Government Scholarship. He was in-charge of overseeing concrete and construction materials with JKR and was also involved in the ASEAN Consultative Committee for Standards and Quality with ABCi. His experience under the Ministry of Development included a position as a resource person for material science under the committee of science and technology. He is also a team member of the Ministry of Development office in fast tracking projects as the Istana Project Supervisor.

    MR. PHILIP FORREST

    Mr. Philip Forrest is the non-Executive Chairman of Readymix and Ready Venture. He is also a non-executive director of several Singapore Companies, and one ASX-listed company. After a career in banking as Citibank's Country Head in Thailand and ANZ's Head of Asia, he is now a Singapore-based independent company director focused on the Australia and ASEAN commercial relationship. He has been presented with the President's Medal by the Australian Chamber of Commerce, Singapore for his contribution to the ASEAN and Australia business relations. He is also an Honorary Fellow of the Australian Institute for Business and Economics.

    MR. NICHOLAS CAMPBELL COCKS

    Mr. Nicholas Cocks is a Founder and a Director of Readymix. Nick started his career in 1985 as an accountant with Arthur Andersen Co in Sydney, Australia. He left the profession in 1989 to join his father's readymix concrete business in Adelaide, Australia. Six months later they sold the company to Adelaide Brighton Cement and Nick turned his attention to South East Asia. He has lived and worked in the region since, developing business in Australia, Brunei, Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore and Vietnam.

    MR. JONATHON CAMPBELL COCKS

    Mr. Jonathon Cocks is a Founder and Director of Readymix. Jon studied Mechanical Engineering, and later Robotics, at Sydney University and joined Readymix in 1994. He has held roles in Operations, IT, Accounting, Finance and Project Management and has lived and worked in South East Asia since 1994 developing businesses in Australia, Brunei, Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore and Vietnam.

    MR. YAP CHIN TEK

    Mr. Yap Chin Tek initially joined the Readymix team in 1985 as its first Quality Control Manager and advanced into the position as the leading General Manager in 1999. Mr. Yap has had over 36 years of experience in the field of concrete when he was first involved with Readymix's sister company in Malaysia, Sun Mix Concrete Sdn. Bhd. His first involvement in one of Brunei Darussalam's major project was Universiti Brunei Darussalam (UBD) which is currently Brunei's largest university. He has overseen the production and distribution of more than 5,000,000 m3 of concrete throughout the nation since his involvement.
